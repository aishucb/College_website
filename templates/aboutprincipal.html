Principal's Message

Technical Education is the backbone of every nation and is the stepping stone for a country to move on in to a developed nation. Holy Grace Academy of Engineering is contributing in the mission of transforming the student to become an engineer with innovation, creativity, human intelligence and patience. “Service to human being is service to God” It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to the creative world of HGAE which has very eco-friendly campus and is well equipped with labs, workshops and libraries to help students in attaining higher standards in academic and professional skills. Creating better human beings is our motto. The main focus of the establishment is to empower students with sound knowledge, wisdom, experience and training both at the academic level of engineering and at the highly competitive global industrial market. Firm determination, hard work and discipline are inevitable for success. You can be assured that you will be proud of yourself as a confident and successful technocrat after four years at HGA*, Mala. Our aim is to make a good citizen and good engineer. I wish all our students and members of faculty a successful and rewarding career.
