About HGAE

ABOUT INSTITUTE

Holy Grace Group of Educational Institutions came into being in 1999 under the banner ‘Holy Grace Foundation’, a registered charitable and educational society formed with an intension to mould the new generation adaptable to the new millennium. Holy Grace which is committed to ‘God and country’ aims to groom a new generation of highly qualified and ethically driven professionals who will serve society in new and innovative ways through their creative ideas and executions. The Holy Grace Academy of Engineering is situated at Mala, Thrissur with more than 81,000 Square feet of built-in space. Located just 19 kms away from Kochi International Airport, Holy Grace Academy of Engineering is away from the hustle and bustle of the city amidst a lush green and tranquil environment, offering the perfect setting for a learning environment that is truly inspirational. The B. Tech program it offers, is a complete industry endorsed package with the university syllabus at its core and all that the industry needs strategically bound around as super competency add-on modules. In terms of content and design, the B. Tech program of Holy Grace Academy of Engineering is packed with many specialties and differences. A stress on innovation is the foundation of the programme which will help students to equip themselves with creative thinking and idea generation as well as about the practical aspects of translating those ideas into practice. Holy Grace Academy of Engineering strives to develop students to become innovators and thought leaders by imparting a rigorous training programme that utilizes academic, co-curricular, personality development, and the latest pedagogical and technological skills. “Education is not learning of facts, but training of the mind to think” -Albert Einstein Placement Department is highly dynamic and has been efficient in placing students into profiles of their choice. The Placement Department is headed by Dean-Placement and ably supported by Assistant Placement Officers, Staff and Student Coordinators. The department monitors the employment opportunities and arranges campus interviews for the final year students. It is designed to function with a high degree of professionalism. It provides almost instantaneous data to the corporate sector with regard to the candidates available for consideration towards placement in accordance with the preserved requirements. The Team interacts with the reputed organizations all over the country for arranging campus interviews for the final year students. We make efforts to organize technical seminars, workshops and corporate expectation sessions. Industry personnel are invited periodically to enrich the knowledge of our student community with the latest technological innovations and industry practices. Our Campus recruitment program starts at the commencement of the 7th semester and keeps continuing till the end of the academic year. Students are also given an opportunity to participate in Off- Campus drives.

HOLY GRACE IDEA HUB

A Start-up and Entrepreneurship Hub of Holy Grace Academy of Engineering endeavors to fetch together the young budding engineer’s entrepreneurial passions to plaster in a common display place. We have espoused a meticulous travel headed to build up primary generation entrepreneurs through consciousness programmes. We bring together aspiring entrepreneurs and support them by providing necessary resources such as mentoring, consultancy and networking. Ultimately, we make them job providers rather than job seekers.

TARGETS

  • • To trigger off the students to imagine innovatively and make inventive ideas from all fields of engineering.
  • • To make over novel ideas into socially and industrially significant products.
  • • To indoctrinate a way of life of innovation-driven entrepreneurship.
  • • Promote knowledge-based and technology-driven Start-ups by harnessing young minds and their innovation potential in today’s world.
  • • To inculcate the spirit of Innovation and Entrepreneurship amongst the young students of Holy Grace Group of Institutions.
  • • To encourage and support start-up creation through mentorship, expertise, and networking.
