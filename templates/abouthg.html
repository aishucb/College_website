About HGAE

Holy Grace Group of Educational Institutions came into being in 1999 under the banner ‘Holy Grace Foundation’, a registered charitable and educational society formed with an intension to mould the new generation adaptable to the new millennium. Holy Grace which is committed to ‘God and country’ aims to groom a new generation of highly qualified and ethically driven professionals who will serve society in new and innovative ways through their creative ideas and executions. The Holy Grace Academy of Engineering is situated at Mala, Thrissur with more than 81,000 Square feet of built-in space. Located just 19 kms away from Kochi International Airport, Holy Grace Academy of Engineering is away from the hustle and bustle of the city amidst a lush green and tranquil environment, offering the perfect setting for a learning environment that is truly inspirational. The B. Tech program it offers, is a complete industry endorsed package with the university syllabus at its core and all that the industry needs strategically bound around as super competency add-on modules. In terms of content and design, the B. Tech program of Holy Grace Academy of Engineering is packed with many specialties and differences. A stress on innovation is the foundation of the programme which will help students to equip themselves with creative thinking and idea generation as well as about the practical aspects of translating those ideas into practice. Holy Grace Academy of Engineering strives to develop students to become innovators and thought leaders by imparting a rigorous training programme that utilizes academic, co-curricular, personality development, and the latest pedagogical and technological skills.
